  helm install my-release . \

To apply the manifests to the Rancher management cluster:
  helm template my-release . --set clusterName=my-k3s-cluster | kubectl apply -f -

Or install the chart into the fleet-default namespace (if your kubectl context is the Rancher management cluster):
  helm install my-release . --set clusterName=my-k3s-cluster -n fleet-default --create-namespace

Then check cluster status in Rancher UI: Cluster Management > your cluster name.
